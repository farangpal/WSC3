FAQ

What is Wonder Secret Club?
Wonder Secret Club is an art-based project born from the fusion of a nostalgic worldview and web3. The story is about a group of kids who are good friends and take on a mysterious case in the fictional town of Fortville (FORTVILL). The emphasis is on story and setting, including nostalgic and mysterious developments.
How many WSC NFTs in the collection.

Which blockchain is used by WSC NFT collections?
WSC NFT collections uses the Ethereum blockchain.
